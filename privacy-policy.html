Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how iByte Hosting LLC ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our game server hosting services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently and in accordance with applicable US law.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and payment details.
  • Payment Information: Billing address and payment method data (processed securely by third-party providers).
  • Usage Data: Server configuration, IP addresses, logs, error reports, and bandwidth usage.
  • Support Communications: Content of your messages and support tickets.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To process payments, invoices, and refunds.
  • To respond to support requests and enforce our Terms and Conditions.
  • To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, or illegal activity.
  • To communicate important updates, service changes, or promotions (with your consent).

3. Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We share data only with trusted service providers (e.g., payment processors, hosting partners) as necessary to deliver our services.
  • We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect our rights and property.
  • In the event of a business transfer (merger, acquisition, or sale), we will notify you before your data is transferred.

4. Data Security

  • We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit (TLS), firewalls, and access controls.
  • We regularly review our security practices and update them as needed.
  • You are responsible for keeping your account credentials secure.

5. Data Retention

  • We retain account and billing records for up to 7 years for tax and legal purposes.
  • Usage logs and backups are kept for up to 1 year, unless required longer by law.
  • Support records are retained for up to 2 years after ticket closure.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • You may request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at privacy@ibytehosting.com.
  • You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

7. Cookies and Tracking

  • We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session, analyze usage, and improve our services.
  • You may disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work as intended.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us for deletion.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post the revised policy with a new effective date. Continued use of our services means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests, contact: privacy@ibytehosting.com
